7.0

Changelog

Metanoia asked us to change the lens. TEDx7.0 asks what you build once the lens has already changed — the ideas that only make sense in hindsight, the pivots that looked like mistakes until they weren't.

Every edition of TEDxMDIGurgaon has been a release, not a rerun: a new theme, a new set of voices, the same red circle. This year's speakers work across real estate, research, design, and the stage itself — brought together for one reason, in the spirit of ideas worth spreading.

Full theme name and speaker bios roll out over the next few weeks. Early access ticket holders see every reveal first.
